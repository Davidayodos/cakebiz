Red Velvet Cake

  • 2 sticks (1 cup or 230g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups (400g) powdered sugar, sifted

PREPARATION INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, baking soda, salt, and cocoa powder.
  2. Combine Wet Ingredients: In another large bowl, whisk together the oil, buttermilk, eggs, food coloring, vinegar, and vanilla extract.
  3. Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients: Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until just combined.
  4. Bake: Divide the batter evenly between the prepared pans. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then turn them out onto wire racks to cool completely.
  5. Beat Cream Cheese and Butter: In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and butter together until light and fluffy.
  6. Decorate: If desired, garnish with cake crumbs or additional decorations.
